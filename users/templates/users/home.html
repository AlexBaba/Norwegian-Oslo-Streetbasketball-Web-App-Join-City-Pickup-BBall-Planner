About
  Intro
Welcome! This is a simple app and small tool developed to plan and organize outdoor basketball trips to the city courts (in Oslo, Norway). A feature is to give sport athletes, players and friend groups insight into who's coming to play ball and when on which days.
  Background
This is a tool that can be used to see which users (who) want to come to the park and plan to take a trip to a selected court on a chosen day that fits your schedule. How many people have reported to come and play on the given day? Users can get updates on the current number of players who plan to play a given day. Users can get shown how many athletes who will join the Sport park and different selections of ball courts in the city of Oslo, in Norway.
  Feature
Attending players will include an overview of which people (users) who have reported arrival in advance, with just one button click! This simple feature works like an open invitation app. It is also possible to cancel your attendance on a trip at any time, so anyone can see your presence updated.

If a meeting day is not found in feeds list, just create one! A requirement is to add a new day when you plan to come, so more players can join a trip.
